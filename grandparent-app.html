Grandchildren Preference App

For a class project, we were tasked to create an app for seniors ages 65+. My group designed an app that shows grandparents their grandchildren's favourite food and activities. This app eliminates questions grandparents may have about their grandchildren's preferences, providing an easier babysitting time.

Tools: Figma, ProtoPie

Skills: UX/UI, ethnographic research, wireframing prototyping

ideation

My group started by brainstorming problems or difficulties our target audience faced. Once we decided on the idea, we sketched ideas for the app's interface.
